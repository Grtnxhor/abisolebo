About Me

Welcome to my page

My name is ABIODUN ABOLAJI SOLEBO mostly known as ABI SOLEBO I graduated in chemistry in 1983 and worked briefly as a Chemical analyst before getting married. Relocated to United Kingdom, and studied Mental Health Nursing. I worked as Mental health Clinical Commissioning manager with different Clinical Commissioning group, and currently work as a part time Self Employed Commission Nurse.

I'm an inspirational writer, gospel songwriter and motivational speaker. I love coaching and empowering especially women to achieve purpose. I am also an Entrepreneur- a lady with many hats :)
